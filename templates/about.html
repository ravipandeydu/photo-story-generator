About StoryLens

What is StoryLens?

StoryLens is an AI-powered application that transforms your photos into creative short stories or poems. Simply upload any photo, and our advanced AI will analyze the image and generate a unique narrative inspired by what it sees. You can then listen to your story with our AI-generated voice narration.

How It Works

StoryLens uses state-of-the-art AI models to understand your images and create engaging narratives:

Image Understanding

We use microsoft/kosmos-2, a multimodal large language model that can understand both images and text. Kosmos-2 analyzes the content, context, and details of your photo to create a meaningful story.

Voice Narration

For audio narration, we use coqui/xtts-v2, an advanced text-to-speech model that produces natural-sounding voices. This model creates high-quality audio that brings your story to life.

Privacy & Data

We value your privacy. The photos you upload are only used to generate stories and are not permanently stored on our servers. All images are automatically deleted after processing.

Technical Details

StoryLens is built with:

  • Python and Flask for the web application
  • Transformers library for AI model integration
  • TTS (Text-to-Speech) library for audio generation
  • Bootstrap for responsive design
